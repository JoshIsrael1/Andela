About

    Josh Osajie Atoyeje (Yoruba/Edo, b. 1995) is a photographer & filmmaker based in Lagos, Nigeria. His work focuses mostly on music, portraiture and documentary. Josh has a BSc in Communications and Multimedia Design (TV/Film) from American University of Nigeria & an MA in Filmmaking (Cinematography) from Goldsmiths University of London. Josh has worked with a significant number of celebrities/organizations including Lagos State, NURHI - A five-year project funded by the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation, YBNL Entertainment, TooLoud Entertainment (Not3s), Banku Music (Mr Eazi), Davido Music Worldwide (Mayorkun), Bahd Guys Records, Tinny Entertainment (Ycee Zaheer), Trace Naija, Chocolate City Music e.t.c. Josh has also done different projects including an ethnographic research/documentation of the Boko Haram internally displaced people in northeast Nigeria. His work has been showcased on different media platforms.
